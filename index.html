Server Rules

"So in everything, do to others what you would have them do to you." Treat all members with kindness and respect. No hate speech, discrimination, or harassment is allowed.
"Do not let any unwholesome talk come out of your mouths, but only what is helpful for building others up according to their needs." Keep language and content clean. No swearing, inappropriate images, or offensive material.
"If it is possible, as far as it depends on you, live at peace with everyone." Avoid arguments, drama, and divisive debates. Discussions should be constructive and loving.
"For the time will come when people will not put up with sound doctrine. Instead, to suit their own desires, they will gather around them a great number of teachers to say what their itching ears want to hear." Only promote teachings that align with the Bible. If unsure, ask an admin or moderator.
"A gossip betrays a confidence, but a trustworthy person keeps a secret." Do not share personal information, including your own or others'. Keep private conversations private.
"Do not lie to each other, since you have taken off your old self with its practices." No lying, misleading, or spreading false information. Honor truth in all interactions.
"Do nothing out of selfish ambition or vain conceit. Rather, in humility value others above yourselves." Refrain from self-promotion, advertisements, or spamming messages, links, or bots.
"Have confidence in your leaders and submit to their authority, because they keep watch over you as those who must give an account." Follow instructions given by staff. Moderators are here to keep the server safe and enjoyable.
"Therefore encourage one another and build each other up, just as in fact you are doing." Be supportive and encouraging. Share God’s love and help others grow in faith.
"Let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven." Use this server to share positivity, Scripture, and prayers. Be a beacon of Christ’s love.
